Privacy Policy

When you log an inventory, your test results are stored on Google's servers, specifically in an "App Engine Datastore." The results are stored with an identifier that is unique to your Google account. This is done so that this program can work properly, and your data persist across devices and time. If you are uncomfortable storing your test results on Google's servers, please do not use this program. I am investigating a method to encrypt the data for an added level of security.

I will never use your information for any purpose that directly identifies you. I may, at some point, and likely for academic purposes, anonymize the database and use it for statistical analysis for any number of ends, but there will never any tie to you personally.
